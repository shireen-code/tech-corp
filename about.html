About Us

Shared Services

IAMTECH CORP. helps our clients design, deploy and deliver and strategically expand high-quality, high value IT, HR, Finance and other Shared Services to improve performance and efficiency throughout government. We help transform the way government delivers service internally by leveraging our Shared Services offering to realize:

  • Improved service delivery with innovative end-to-end functional and technical business process solutions
  • Replication of best practices enterprise-wide through the use of standardized processes
  • Enhanced opportunities for identifying and economically implementing enabling technology
  • Significant economies of scale can be reached through creative (less hierarchical) organization design

DEFENSE

IAMTECH CORP. is committed to delivering IT and mission support services to help our Defense customers enhance the safety and security of our nation. By leveraging innovative IT solutions and domain expertise, we support these critical operations and deliver systems and services that improve mission performance in areas such as command and control, cyber security, logistics, and enterprise IT networks. Our work supports some of our country’s most important national security programs.

  • Manage worldwide networks for Navy and Marine Corps forces
  • Deliver a full range of IT services for our military

We Offer

  • Leading innovation and excellence in advanced technologies
  • Delivery of core and Next-Gen IT services across broad customer base with lower incremental cost
  • Leadership in IT and professional services across DoD and in ERP delivery for military services and Defense agencies
